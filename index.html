About WIF-Pay｜關於 WIF-Pay

          

            WIF-Pay is not a payment processor. It is a responsibility replay layer for payment events.

            WIF-Pay 不是支付處理器，而是付款事件的責任回放層。
          

          

            It does not replace banks, payment platforms, or service providers. It converts payment events into traceable, auditable, and replayable responsibility chains.

            它不取代銀行、不取代平台、不取代服務商。它把付款事件轉換成可追溯、可審計、可回放的責任鏈。
